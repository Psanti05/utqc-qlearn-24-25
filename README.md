# utqc-qlearn-24-25
Welcome to qLearn, the University of Toronto Quantum Computing Association's Introductory Quantum Computing lecture series!

## Overview
This lecture series is designed to provide participants with a foundational understanding of quantum computing. We will cover key concepts, delve into the mathematical underpinnings, and explore the practical implications of quantum algorithms in real-world applications.



## Contact
For any questions or further information, please reach out to the organizing committee at [m.silver@mail.utoronto.ca](mailto:m.silver@mail.utoronto.ca). We look forward to exploring the fascinating world of quantum algorithms with you!
